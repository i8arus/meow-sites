CATS

Cats are small, carnivorous mammals that have been companions to humans for thousands of years. Known for their grace, independence, and playful curiosity, cats belong to the species Felis catus and are one of the most popular pets worldwide. They are admired for their sleek bodies, sharp reflexes, and unique personalities, ranging from affectionate and cuddly to mysterious and aloof. Domesticated cats can adapt to a variety of environments and often form close bonds with their human families, while still retaining many of the instincts of their wild ancestors. With their quiet charm and captivating behavior, cats continue to fascinate and comfort people across cultures and generations.

PROS OF HAVING A CAT

Built-in therapist (with fur) Feeling sad? Your cat will sit near you—not too close—and judge your life choices silently until you feel better. Pest control expert Who needs a professional when you have a tiny assassin that hunts bugs, shadows, and the occasional sock? Alarm clock with attitude They’ll wake you up at 4 AM by gently sitting on your face or knocking things off your table. You're welcome.

CONS OF HAVING A CAT

  • You now live with a furry little dictator
  • They don’t care about your mental health
  • You're a prisoner in your own home
  • Fur becomes a personality trait
  • They treat affection like emotional manipulation
